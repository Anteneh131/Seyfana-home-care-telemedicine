About Us

Seyfana Home Care & Telemed is a digital healthcare service provided by Seyfana Primary Hospital. Our mission is to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare professionals through technology, compassion, and personalized care. We serve individuals and families who seek professional medical services in the comfort of their homes.

Core Services

  • Doctor home visits
  • Online medical consultations via phone or video
  • Diagnostic sample collection at home
  • Medication delivery & prescription management
  • Maternal & child care services
  • Elderly & chronic disease follow-up care
  • Post-surgical nursing support
  • Nutrition and lifestyle coaching

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced and certified medical professionals
  • Convenience and comfort of receiving care at home
  • Timely follow-up and continuous support
  • Affordable service packages
  • Secure telemedicine platform compliant with Ethiopian health policies

FAQs

1. Do I need internet for telemedicine?
Yes, for video calls. Phone consultations are also available.

2. Are the services available 24/7?
Emergency and palliative care services are available round the clock. General services are provided during business hours.

3. Can I book for someone else?
Yes, you can book for a family member or dependent.

Languages

We offer services in Amharic, Afaan Oromoo, and English.
